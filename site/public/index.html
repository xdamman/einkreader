einkreader

A calm, offline-first reading app for e-ink tablets.

Download the APK Free & open source · Android · built for e-ink

Everything you want to read, in one quiet place

All your sources

RSS feeds, Twitter bookmarks, Nostr profiles — notes, long reads and bookmarks — organized in folders. Share any link into the app or paste one; it downloads to read later.

Truly offline

Articles and images download in full at sync. Threads are stitched together whole. Read on a plane, in a park, wherever — nothing needs a connection.

Files you own

Your whole library is plain Markdown in a clear year/source folder structure, plus a single editable highlights.md. Back up, sync, or browse it with any app — no lock-in, ever.

Highlight & note

Select text to highlight, attach a private note, and find everything again in one place. Your reading position is remembered per article.

Share on your terms

Private and local-first by default. If you want, create a public profile and share chosen highlights — to your followers, by email, or as a quote tweet. You pick what to share, every time.

Made for e-ink

High-contrast black & white, no animations to smear, discrete page refreshes, and layouts tuned for tablets like the iFLYTEK AINOTE and Boox devices.
